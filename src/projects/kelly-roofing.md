## The Brief

Kelly Roofing needed a commercial presence that felt trustworthy without being generic. The goal was to stand apart from the sea of contractor ads that all look and sound the same — stock footage, bold claims, forgettable.

## Approach

We focused on real footage, real crews, and real neighborhoods. The edit was designed to move quickly — matching the pace of social feeds — while still telling a clear story about reliability and craftsmanship.

Every shot was chosen to communicate competence: clean compositions of crews at work, detail shots of materials, and wide establishing shots that ground the work in recognizable communities.

## Result

A versatile commercial package that works across broadcast, social, and digital — designed to be cut down or extended depending on placement. Built for performance, not just aesthetics.
